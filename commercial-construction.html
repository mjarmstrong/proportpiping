Technology

We are the first in Alberta, and among fewer than five in Canada, to adopt this revolutionary layout technology for commercial construction layout. After 2.5 years of in-depth research and study, we are now equipped with a game-changing solution that redefines the way construction layout is performed through advanced surveying methods. Leveraging state-of-the-art layout systems, our methods simplify complex layouts, reduce errors, and accelerate project timelines. This groundbreaking technology ensures unparalleled accuracy and boosts productivity transforming how construction layouts are executed.

Applications

The technology utilized has a wide array of jobsite applications, and benefits including:

  • Pre-construction as-builts
  • BIM (Building Information Model)
  • Virtual design construction
  • Quality control during construction
  • Comparing design intent to As-built
  • Pre-fabrication of all spools
  • Estimating inch counts in minutes
