What is Construction Layout?

Construction layout is the process of transferring design plans onto a physical construction site to guide accurate building placement. Traditionally, this involves manual staking and marking, but HP SitePrint, a robotic layout solution, automates this process for greater speed, precision, and efficiency.

What is HP SitePrint?

HP SitePrint is a robotic layout printer that autonomously prints construction layouts on-site based on digital CAD or BIM files. Rather than relying on chalk lines, tape measures, and manual interpretation of drawings, SitePrint allows teams to automate and speed up the layout process with robotic precision.

HP SitePrint is revolutionizing commercial construction layout by improving speed, accuracy, and cost efficiency, making it an ideal solution for high-rise buildings, industrial facilities, and complex job sites.
