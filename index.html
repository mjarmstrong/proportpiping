Who We Are

Proport Piping Design is a leading oil and gas facility design company specializing in advanced 3D scanning technology. Our expertise lies in scanning or pre-scanning of sites and equipment using 5k full scans, empowering clients to optimize asset management, reduce project costs, and enhance safety measures.

What We Do

3D Scanning

Our expedient data capture and unparalleled accuracy virtually eliminates the risk of errors and by minimizing the amount of personnel and site visits greatly reduces safety infractions. Drawing in the 3D point cloud, we have the ability to see conflicts between your projects current design, and existing facilities, piping and structures before your team reaches site.

Cost Reduction and Time Savings

Through virtual site visits and accurate measurements, Proport reduces the need for costly on-site visits, streamlines project planning, and improves construction efficiency, ultimately saving time and resources.

Safety and Compliance

Utilize our scanning technology to ensure compliance with safety regulations, assess potential hazards remotely, and plan for safe working conditions during shutdowns and turnarounds.

Enhanced Asset Management

Optimize asset utilization and sustainability by leveraging our precise scanning technology to gather comprehensive site and equipment data, enabling better decision-making and frugal asset reuse.

Our Services

Site Scanning and Virtual Visits

Conduct virtual, photo-realistic visits to project sites using our 5k scan photos, enabling remote assessments and site evaluations for construction and safety requirements. Measure distances, heights, and access points without the need for physical presence.

Piping Design and Elimination of Errors

Our skilled scanning crew eliminates costly pipefitting measurement errors by transposing engineered piping into the 3D scanned point cloud model. This ensures conflict resolutions with existing structures and equipment, streamlining fabrication and installation processes.

Data Hosting and Sharing

Store and organize project data efficiently with Proport Piping Design. Utilize an intuitive interface to share point cloud data with contractors, providing temporary access throughout the project lifecycle without the need for CAD or 3D skills.
