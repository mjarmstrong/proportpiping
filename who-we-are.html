Who We Are

Why Choose Us

Proport Piping is a leading oil and gas facility design company specializing in advanced 3D laser scanning technology. We are dedicated to providing top-quality design solutions for a wide range of industries. With years of experience and a commitment to excellence, we have established ourselves as a trusted partner in the field of oil and gas facility design.

Meet the owner

My name is Zachary McNeil. I would love to introduce myself to you. I have a wealth of experience in the oil and gas industry, specifically in the field of pipefitting. Over my 14-year career, I have not only honed my skills in practical pipefitting but I have also taken on significant supervisory roles on large-scale projects across British Columbia, Alberta, and Saskatchewan. This has given me a robust understanding of the industry's demands and intricacies.

My transition into the design aspect of pipefitting and construction marks a significant evolution in my career. By working closely with engineering firms and construction companies, I leverage advanced technologies like 3D laser scanning and software tools such as Cadworx and Plant 3D. My expertise allows me to create precise as-built facilities, reducing the need for costly change orders and Requests for Information (RFIs). Essentially, I can serve as a crucial link between the conceptual design and the practical implementation of projects, ensuring that potential issues are addressed proactively.

My role involves a blend of hands-on experience and technical design skills, positioning me as an invaluable resource in mitigating project risks and enhancing efficiency. This combination of field expertise and advanced technological proficiency not only alleviates headaches for our clients but also contributes to the overall success and cost-effectiveness of the projects we undertake.
